What is it?

Android: NetRunner is a Living Card Game set in a dystopian cyber-noir future. In this assymetrical two-player card game, each person takes on the role of a Runner or a Corp. The Runner is a hacker that is trying to get into the Corp's servers to steal their agendas to win. The Corp has protect their servers with cards called ICE while trying to score out agendas. The first player to 7 agenda points wins. The Corp can also win by flatlining the Runner through meat damage, net damage, or brain damage.

Runner and Corp Factions

The game pits Runner versus Corp. There are 3 Runner factions (Anarchs, Criminals, Shapers) and 4 Corp factions (Haas-Bioroid, Jinteki, NBN, Weyland Consortium). In addition to the two sides, each faction within that side has their own unique play style. You can read more about the factions above.
